Pawsome friends of Paw Patrol

Ryder is a 10-year-old boy and the leader of the PAW Patrol. He was voiced by Owen Mason from Season 1-Mid Season 2, by Elijha Hammill from Late Season 2-Mid Season 3, Jaxon Mercey from Late Season 3-Mid Season 6, and by Joey Nijem from late-Season 6 onward.

Marshall is a clumsy but competent Dalmatian who serves as a firefighter and paramedic dog. He was voiced by Gage Munroe from Season 1, by Drew Davis from Season 2-season 5, by Lukas Engel from 2019 to 2020, and by Kingsley Marshall from 2020 onward.

Chase is a German Shepherd who serves as a police and spy dog, who also has a cat and feather allergy. He was voiced by Tristan Samuel from Season 1, by Max Calinescu from Season 2-Season 4, and by Justin Paul Kelly from Season 5 onward.
